Right and Wrong

A Guide You Can Trust

How do you decide what is right and
what is wrong? Many people rely on their
conscience and the values that they have
learned. Some make decisions based
on what other people think. What guides
your decisions about right and wrong?
Can you be sure that the decisions you
make will result in the best future for you
and your family?
